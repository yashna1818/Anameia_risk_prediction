Welcome to the Anemia Risk Prediction & Precision Nutrition Platform

Leakage-Free XGBoost Architecture

Unlike traditional pipelines that leak complete blood count (CBC) parameters to predict hemoglobin, our pipeline enforces strict data separation. All CBC parameters (Hematocrit, RDW, MCH, MCV, RBC count) are dropped, preventing logical leakage and forcing the model to discover deep relationships between genetics, demographics, and diet.

XGBoost Only Algorithms
100% Leakage-Free

VCF Genomics & PRS

Upload standard patient VCF files. The system instantly parses genetic variants, cross-references them against our 400-SNP GWAS significance database, and computes an individualized Polygenic Risk Score (PRS) in the browser, classifying genetic susceptibility into Low, Moderate, or High risk cohorts.

Pathway-Burden Analysis

Instead of providing general recommendations, our engine aggregates genetic variants into 10 biological pathway burden axes (e.g., Hepcidin Regulation, Duodenal Iron Reduction, RBC Membrane Lipids). Recommendations are directly linked to the specific molecular pathways that are genetically compromised.

Precision Nutrition Engine

Calculates nutrient gaps, identifies dietary conflicts (such as iron-sensing overload mutations combined with dietary deficiency), and designs targeted meal plans including exact quantities, timing restrictions (like tannin blockage), and vitamin C pairings.
